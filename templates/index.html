Agriculture

Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock. It includes the preparation of plant and animal products for people to use and their distribution to markets.Agriculture provides most of the world’s food and fabrics. Cotton, wool, and leather are all agricultural products. Agriculture also provides wood for construction and paper products. These products, as well as the agricultural methods used, may vary from one part of the world to another.

About

The phrase “Right Place, Right Time, Right Product” sums up precision farming. This is more accurate and controlled technique that replaces the repetitive and labourintensive part of farming.The “Smart Agriculture System” its main objective is to predict the most profitable crop in the forecasted weather and soil conditions at a specified location on the bases of current environmental conditions.



This system will reduce the damage and the hard work of farmers caused by growing crops which is not suitable at that location. This system also provides farmer friendly environment in which farmers can have their queries solved by simply asking questions.
